# Terms of Use

**Last updated: 2026-07-19**

FastMask is an unofficial, open-source client for [Fastmail](https://www.fastmail.com) masked email addresses, developed by **Paweł Orzech** (sole proprietorship "YesWas", contact: [pawel@orzech.me](mailto:pawel@orzech.me)). It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Fastmail Pty Ltd.

## 1. The app

FastMask lets you manage your own Fastmail masked email addresses using your own Fastmail API token. The app talks directly to Fastmail's servers; there is no FastMask backend. Your use of Fastmail itself is governed by Fastmail's own terms.

The app's source code is available under the MIT License at [github.com/pawelorzech/FastMask](https://github.com/pawelorzech/FastMask).

## 2. FastMask Pro

FastMask Pro is an optional, **one-time in-app purchase** processed by Google Play. It permanently unlocks cosmetic and convenience features (accent themes, biometric app lock, CSV export) on the Google account used for the purchase. It is **not a subscription** — there are no recurring charges.

- The price is shown in the app before purchase, in your local currency, as provided by Google Play.
- The purchase is tied to your Google account and can be restored on any device using that account ("Restore purchase" on the Pro screen).
- Refunds are handled through Google Play according to [Google Play's refund policy](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637).
- All existing free features remain free; Pro only adds extras.
